Histry of P.M.V


90 km from Trincomalee city and 110 km from Anuradhapura city, the school is located in the Padavi Sripura Divisional Secretariat Division and Sewajanapada GS Division.
Since the beginning of the Padavi Sripura Education Division, which consists of 12 schools, the main school is T/TN Padavi Palugahawanguwa school. Also, due to the location of the area limiting Mullaitivu and Thiriaya forest at a distance of about eight kilometers, the people had to experience the bitter experiences of thirty years of war.


This college started on the 5th of January 1961 under the leadership of Padavi Sripura Rajamaha Viharaya Honorable Pihibiya Gollewe Dhammaloka Thero and has become a center for educating and imparting education to the sons and daughters of the farmers who were the founders of Padaviya movement.


This is the first college established in Padavi Sri Pura division and initially started offering classes up to 8th grade.
In 1964, ten children appeared for the general examination and for the first time, four children passed in 1968. After that, in 1980, Advanced Level Arts Class and Commerce Class in 1982, started. Nearly 100 number of students have been admitted to the university even in military back ground.


Under the facilities received so far, in the years 2010 and 2011, three and four A/L students were given the opportunity to enter the university, and the O/L results in the years 2010 and 2011 were able to reach a level exceeding 50%.
